## A "Lengthy and Substantive" conversation
According to [The Hill](https://thehill.com/homenews/senate/5957438-mcconnell-thune-barrasso-health/), Senator McConnell spoke with GOP leadership Monday and Tuesday this week.

> “Leader Thune spoke with Sen. McConnell yesterday by phone. 
> They had a lengthy and substantive conversation that covered a 
> variety of topics, including national security,” 
>   - John Thune spokesperson


The phone call lasted around 20 minutes and they caught up on a variety of topics

## Senate Back in Session Next Week

The GOP leaders also discussed the July work period and what's on the docket when they return from break. The clear message from
Senator McConnell's office is that he is actively engaged and eager to return to work when the Senate reconvenes next week. Some
are struggling to reconcile this with his 3+ week hospital stay, but it's impossible to say given the limited details of his
illness.

This statement comes on the heels of last week's transcript of the 911 call to Senator McConnell's residence which paints a 
significantly worse picture.
