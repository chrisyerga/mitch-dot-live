# Where is Mitch McConnell?

While there have been no official statements from his staff since June 22, his security detail was seen at Walter Reed Medical Center where he is receiving medical attention.

Senator McConnell was absent from the Senate the week of June 22nd. The United States Senate is currently in recess until July 13th. Widespread expectations are continued silence from his team unless there is a material change in his condition.

## Online Speculation

Given the lack of communication on the Senator's health, rumors abound online. These include speculation
that the Senator is already deceased and his death is being kept secret to prevent the Democratic Governor of Kentucky from naming a successor. This is patently **false**. The Kentucky General Assembly passed House Bill 622 in 2024, which completely stripped Democratic Governor Andy Beshear of his power to appoint a temporary Senator.

The actual process is this:
- If a Senate seat becomes vacant due to death, resignation, or expulsion, the governor is legally required to call a special election. The people of Kentucky vote to fill the seat, and the winner serves out the remainder of the term.
- The Timeline: By law, a special election to fill a U.S. Senate vacancy must take place not less than 80 days and not more than 150 days after the vacancy

The full text of [HB 622](https://apps.legislature.ky.gov/record/26rs/hb622.html) is available for the studious among you.
